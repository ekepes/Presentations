Why do you want to be a technical leader?

Bad reasons

Money, fame, glory
Being “in charge"

Good reasons

Share your experience
Help other people become leaders

How

Leader != Manager

A Leader is someone people want to follow
Your team will choose you - can’t really force it. To help things along - be the kind of leader you’d want to work with

Do's

Trust your team
Work with your team
Take blame, even when it’s not your fault
Stay relevant/Stay technical
Say, “I don’t know, let’s find out"
